# Original labyrinth demo, migrated to the new DSL.
# To play this instead of game.txt, point parser.ts at this file.

@title The Labyrinth

@character explorer right danielNotTalking.png danielTalking.png
@character wanderer left  melNotTalking.png    melTalking.png
@narrator forest

@background day     cloudyBackground.png
@background night   nightBackground.png
@background sunrise sunriseBackground.png


::start START
@wanderer
wanderer: How long have I been wandering through this labyrinth? Every corridor looks exactly the same...
--Continue -> keepGoing


::keepGoing
cb night
wanderer: I can't stop now. The lights are getting dimmer.
--Continue -> explorer


::explorer
@wanderer
explorer: HEY! You there!
--Continue -> explorerSeesYou


::explorerSeesYou
@wanderer
explorer: Wait... are you another explorer?
--Continue -> explorerIntro


::explorerIntro
@wanderer
explorer: Finally! I've been trying to find the exit for hours.
--Work together         -> planRoute
--Ignore and walk away  -> lost


::planRoute
@wanderer
explorer: I think the moonlight is showing us the safer paths. What do you think?
--Follow the moonlight        -> deeper
--Head toward the sound of water -> lost


::deeper
@wanderer
explorer: The air feels colder here... I think we're getting closer.
--Keep going -> strangeSound


::lost
@wanderer
explorer: I don't recognize this part of the maze...
* A low growl echoes through the corridor...
--Investigate the sound -> guardian


::strangeSound
@wanderer
* GRRRR...
--Turn around -> guardian


::guardian
@wanderer
* MWAHAHA. To leave this labyrinth, you must prove your worth.
--Continue -> trial1


::trial1
@wanderer
* First trial: Name one thing a good explorer must always carry.
-? map -> trial1Correct / trial1Incorrect


::trial1Correct
@wanderer
* Acceptable. Preparation is key.
--Continue -> trial2


::trial1Incorrect
@wanderer
* Incorrect. You are unprepared.
--Return -> lost


::trial2
@wanderer
* Second trial: Solve my riddle. I guide but never speak, I move but have no feet. What am I?
-? compass -> trial2Correct / trial2Incorrect


::trial2Correct
@wanderer
* Hmmm. You are wiser than most.
--Continue -> trial3


::trial2Incorrect
@wanderer
* Wrong. Try again.
-? compass -> trial2Correct / trial2Incorrect


::trial3
@wanderer
* Final trial: What disappears the moment you say its name?
-? silence -> trial3Correct / trial3Incorrect


::trial3Correct
@wanderer
* Impressive...
--Proceed -> ending1


::trial3Incorrect
@wanderer
* Not quite.
--Try again -> trial3


::ending1
@wanderer
* No one has passed my trials in centuries.
--Continue -> ending2


::ending2
@wanderer
* Very well. The labyrinth recognizes your skill.
--Continue -> ending3


::ending3
cb sunrise
* You may leave.
--Step into the light -> end


::end
:!
