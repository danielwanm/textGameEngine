# ----------------------------------------------------------------------------
# Sample game: "The Wayfarer's Trial"
#
# This script demonstrates every feature of the engine:
#   - title and background declarations
#   - two characters on opposite sides
#   - a narrator (no sprite, full-width text box)
#   - multi-line nodes with sequential speakers
#   - branching options
#   - text-input puzzles with right/wrong branches
#   - mid-game background changes
#   - a terminal node
# ----------------------------------------------------------------------------

@title The Wayfarer's Trial

@character hero    right danielNotTalking.png danielTalking.png
@character witch   left  melNotTalking.png    melTalking.png
@narrator narrator

@background day     cloudyBackground.png
@background night   nightBackground.png
@background sunrise sunriseBackground.png


# --- Opening: narrator-only node sets the scene ---------------------------
::open START
cb day
* A traveler crests the final hill. The road ends at an old stone arch.
--Step through the arch -> meet


# --- A node with two speakers in sequence (click to advance between) ----
::meet
cb night
@witch
hero: Who's there? I thought this place was abandoned.
witch: Abandoned, perhaps. Unguarded — never.
--Ask who she is        -> identify
--Draw your sword       -> hostile


# --- Branching: choosing 'identify' leads to a puzzle -------------------
::identify
witch: I am the keeper of this gate. Answer my riddle and you may pass.
* The witch raises a withered hand.
--I'll try -> riddle


# --- Input puzzle: matches if answer CONTAINS "compass" -----------------
::riddle
witch: I guide but never speak, I move but have no feet. What am I?
-? compass -> passed / wrongAnswer


::wrongAnswer
witch: No. Think again, traveler.
-? compass -> passed / wrongAnswer


# --- The other branch: hostility loops back to the puzzle ---------------
::hostile
witch: Foolish. Steel cannot answer what the gate asks.
--Lower your weapon -> identify


# --- Success path: background change + terminal node -------------------
::passed
cb sunrise
witch: Correct. The needle, the lodestone, the silent guide.
hero: Then the road is mine?
witch: For now.
--Walk on -> ending


::ending
* Dawn breaks. The arch fades behind you as the next valley opens below.
:!
